WE PRESERVE NATURE

One of the important principles of our hotels is environmental friendliness. We preserve nature in its original form, taking care of the surrounding world. Considering that construction in protected areas is prohibited, and nature must remain untouched, we have installed beautiful glampings on the seashore with all the amenities of civilization. After all, today residents of noisy cities and megacities are increasingly choosing a calm and measured rest somewhere in nature. And if you suddenly want to be on the edge of the earth, we are ready to receive you in a paradise corner - on the shore of the Caspian Sea - at the glamping hotel O'mir Kazakhstan.
